Accelerators
==============


AccelGroup
------

The Gtk.AccelGroup represents a group of keyboard accelerators, typically
attached to a toplevel :class:`Gtk.Window`.

Accelerators are shortcuts for activating a menu item; they appear alongside
the menu item they’re a shortcut for. For example “Ctrl+Q” might appear alongside
the “Quit” menu item. Not to be confused with Mnemonics.
